Amazon Account Manager

Cousin Corporation of America is currently seeking an experienced Amazon Account Manager to add to our growing and evolving Sales Department. The Amazon Account Manager will be responsible for growing our brands' presence on Amazon. The ideal candidate is extremely detail oriented, motivated and a persistent self-starter who strives for data driven results with an excellent attention to detail. This position reports to the Sales Support Manager.

Duties & Responsibilities
  • Develop and manage product content catalog
  • Knowledge and ability to use Amazon template to upload products with variations
  • Create new product listings with associated attributes and descriptions that meet SEO best practices to increase current and new traffic
  • Review detail pages for consistency and accuracy
  • Manage customer reviews, increase good review counts and respond to any negative reviews
  • SKU and UPC management with brand registry
  • Use key metrics in Amazon's ranking algorithm to increase product popularity
  • Create strategies to increase sales on Amazon and analyze performance results
  • Full ownership of a discount/promotional calendar
  • Monitor and analyze ASIN performance, rankings, and ratings
  • Must be able to make data-driven decisions to calculate required fulfillment quantities based on demand, product and market analysis
Knowledge, Skills & Abilities
  • Solid understanding of shipping and fulfillment requirements for online marketplaces
  • Self-starter, able to think analytically and pursue maximum efficiency for data driven results
  • Excellent communication and writing skills with the ability to absorb and apply constructive feedback
  • Experience in women's lifestyle and/or jewelry industry is a big plus
Minimum Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree in Sales, Business, Communications, Marketing or combination of education and work-related experience
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience listing and managing products on E-Commerce platforms
  • Extensive experience with Microsoft Office with advanced Excel skills
  • Proficiency with all of Amazon's paid marketing solutions
Salary & Benefits
  • Competitive salary and bonus
  • Health, Dental, Vision, Life, Disability, 401K, paid vacation and holidays
How to Apply

Please email a resume and a cover letter to tell us about yourself and how your prior work experience would benefit us in this position. Please include your wage history for your last two positions and send to kellycrossan@cousin.com.

Brand Manager

Cousin Corporation of America is currently looking to add a Brand Manager to our growing marketing team! Our brands include Cousin DIY and Laura Janelle and we have relationships with brands such as Cosmopolitan, Swarovski, LaurDIY, DC Comics, Wizard of Oz, and Harry Potter. The Brand Manager will lead all marketing initiatives related to the brands he or she is assigned. The ideal candidate is both highly creative and extremely organized. This positon reports to the Marketing Manager.

Duties & Responsibilities
  • Develop packaging and photography using brand's style guide
  • Provide captivating and engaging content for B2B and B2C brand websites
  • Develop and maintain all social sites for the brands
  • Provide engaging content for various social media channels using brand's style guide
  • Analyze performance of social content to continuously improve traffic and capture more followers
  • Develop social media campaigns for new product launches
  • Write polished press releases
  • Manage the submission of marketing assets through the brand approval platform
  • Develop teaser videos of upcoming product launches for sales reps
  • Produce creative videos for social media and websites
  • Create and edit content for catalogs
  • Lead tradeshow marketing planning to include the development and execution of print ads, e-blasts, and other showroom space marketing materials
Knowledge, Skills & Abilities
  • 3+ years successful experience developing and executing new marketing programs and creating compelling content for catalog and websites
  • Strong time management, organizational and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and writing skills with the ability to absorb and apply constructive feedback
Minimum Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business, Communications, or combination of education and work-related experience
  • 3+ years of experience in branding and marketing
  • Extensive experience with Adobe Creative Suite and Microsoft Office products
Salary & Benefits
  • Competitive salary and bonus
  • Health, Dental, Vision, Life, Disability, 401K, paid vacation and holidays
